Map of the World

A Super Cool Pixel-Style Map of Planet Earth

Trying to squash the entire globe onto a 2D plane for mapping is kind of a large challenge. There's really no perfect way to do this and so compromises have to be made that will result in distortions. Decisions have to be made and most result in imperfect solutions that are a reflection of the map maker's background and biases. Those ridiculous USA-centered maps that chop Russia into two parts are a good example of this. At least with the image below, Greenland isn't the same size as AFrica which, as someone who lives in Africa, is always annoying to see.
